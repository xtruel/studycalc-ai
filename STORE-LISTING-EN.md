## Description (max 4000)
```
StudyCalc AI is the smart calculator that doesn't make the rounding mistakes you see in spreadsheets and ordinary calculators.

It runs on an arbitrary‑precision engine: more than 130 exact decimal places, huge and tiny numbers, long divisions, powers, square roots and percentages — all without the typical floating‑point errors (the classic 0.1 + 0.2 problem simply doesn't happen here).

Type an expression (even in plain words), or point your camera at an exercise: the app reads the text directly on your device and shows you the solution step by step, with a final check of the result.

WHAT YOU CAN DO
• Solve arithmetic expressions with exact fractions, powers, roots and parentheses
• Solve linear and quadratic equations, step by step
• Get exact decimal results with 130+ digits — the true value is always kept
• Read exercises from a photo with on‑device text recognition
• Work in 6 languages (IT/EN/ES/FR/DE/PT) and even type calculations in plain words

WHY IT'S DIFFERENT
• No rounding errors: a real arbitrary‑precision math engine, not 64‑bit floats
• Readable results without breaking the real value behind them
• Clear handling of edge cases: division by zero, invalid input, extreme numbers

PRIVACY FIRST
• 100% offline — no internet connection required
• No account, no ads, no subscription
• Nothing is ever sent to a server: your data stays on your device

Perfect for middle and high‑school students, teachers, and anyone who needs calculations they can trust.
```
